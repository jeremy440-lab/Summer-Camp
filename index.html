Four skills. Four gaps that cost people time.
Giddy Up
Lead through influence
You know when the room is drifting. You go along anyway. This is the practice of asking one question instead.
⏱ Saves time in meetings that drift
Bullseye
Prioritize what matters most
Your list keeps growing. The filter does not tell you to do less. It tells you what to do first.
⏱ Saves time on the wrong targets
Cannonball
Decide with imperfect info
A test has a question. If you cannot name what you are testing for, you already have the answer.
⏱ Saves time decisions sit undecided
Trailhead
Navigate ambiguity
The trail does not get clearer the longer you stand at the trailhead. This is the practice of moving while it is still foggy.
⏱ Saves time waiting for clarity
Nobody posts perfect. They post real.
